# Alby Pro

Alby Pro is a paid subscription that unlocks additional features on top of a free Alby account. It applies to both self-hosted Alby Hub (with a connected Alby account) and Alby Cloud.

Upgrade: https://www.getalby.com/subscription/pro

## Benefits

- **Unlimited Sub-wallets** — Share with friends, family, or coworkers without the free-tier sub-wallet limit
- **Encrypted Dynamic Channel Backups (VSS)** — Secure dynamic wallet backups in the cloud, so lightning and bitcoin state is automatically recoverable (LDK only, and only for brand-new hubs connected to an Alby account with an active subscription)
- **Priority Support** — Get help faster when you need it
- **Custom Lightning Address** — Create your personalized lightning address
- **Email Notifications** — Stay updated on important activity
- …and more
